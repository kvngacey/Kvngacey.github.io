AJ's life

Ajamo or AJ is a Newark, NJ native.Both his parents come from trinidadian descent. I am part of the A Better Chance program which helped him find a more competitive high school. Now I go to a community school called New Canaan High School located in New Canaan, CT. I grew up in rough environments, but has always excelled in school. I attended many catholic schools where I met friends I will have for a lifetime. I now have an entirely different life. Going from black and latino schools to a completely white school was a total culture shock for me. I embrace the challenge and now I am a member of two societys which helps me learn alot. My future career will either be computer science related or social science related. Whatever path I take it will lead me to success.
